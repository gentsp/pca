Mara Residence

New York, US

Mara is a 277-unit, multi-family garden style complex serving Eagle, ID.

Named for Clara Aikens, the daughter of the founder of Eagle, ID – the Clara is a groundbreaking 277-unit multi-family community. The site plan was thoughtfully crafted to highlight the natural beauty of Eagle as well as its direct access to the Boise river and a 5-acre pond that provide unrivaled recreational opportunities for our residents.

Architect: Granit Humolli Landscape: The Lorem Group Inc. Contractor: PCA construction Inc. Website: www.weblink.com
