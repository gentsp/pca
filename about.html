About Us

PCA Pacific Construction Inc. is a leading Canadian contracting company providing overall coordination and management of light commercial and multi- family residential projects.

Operated by a group of individuals with several years of development and construction experience, PCA is driving the transformation of the industry to meet a sustainable future.

Mission

PCA Pacific Construction Inc’s mission is to provide the highest quality of craftsmanship for a sustainable future.

Our success is based on the integrity of our subcontractors and staff combined with our commitment to a strong work ethic while staying up to date with the newest innovations of the industry.

Vision

PCA Pacific Construction Inc. vision is to be an international leading company in the development and construction of sustainable future environments.
